Yonsei University

Mind and Memory
Research Initiative

The Yonsei Mind and Memory Research Initiative (YMM) promotes events providing a platform for scholars working on the philosophy of mind and memory to present their work to audiences at Yonsei University and South Korea.

YMM organizes a Lectures Series, a Workshop Series, and hosts Research Visitors.
